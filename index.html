About me

I'm a junior web designer, developer and a student of 5th year of Applied Computer Science at AGH University. Currently I live in Warsaw, where I develop my skills and look towards new challenges and nice environment to grow into professionalist. I am trying to use my innate curiosity to do what motivates me the most - acquiring knowledge and widening my personal horizons.

Why do I love to do it ?

I love to have a contact with people around me. These days a well designed website is a must for each subject that wants to have a wonderful relationship with their customers on a daily basis. Through the graphical interfaces this goal can be reached. I am satisfied when a person likes a page so much that he or she come back over and over to meet with that design and feelings again.
